Privacy Policy for Finance Tracker

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled in the Finance Tracker application ("the application"). This application is a demonstration project created for portfolio purposes. The goal of this policy is to be transparent about the data this application collects and uses.

2. Information We Collect

The application collects the following types of information solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect a username and a hashed version of your password for authentication purposes. We never store passwords in plain text.
  • User-Generated Content: All financial data you create, such as transactions (descriptions, amounts, types) and categories, is stored in the database to provide the application's services back to you.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To create and maintain your user account.
  • To allow you to log in and securely access your data.
  • To store, process, and display your transaction and category data as part of the application's core features.
  • To operate and maintain the application's functionality.

We do not use your data for any other purpose. It is not used for analytics, marketing, or profiling.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted for this project. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information, such as hashing passwords and using standard security practices. However, please be aware that as a demonstration project, it does not have the same level of security as a commercial, production-grade application.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information or financial data with any third parties. All data collected is used solely within the application.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists within the application. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy is static for the purpose of this demonstration project and is not expected to change.
